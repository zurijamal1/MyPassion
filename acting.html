Acting and Film

My passion is film and theater, and I am passionate about it because I love the art of storytelling through visual arts and being able to transport yourself to another world.

I was introduced to it by watching movies when I was younger, especially Disney movies, and I wanted to be a part of a story or tell my own story through film.

One of my main inspirations in the beginning was Disney because I felt that their acting was cringey and I thought I could do better than them.

The one who continues to inspire me is myself in the future. I imagine myself to be well off and happy in my career and my future, and I know I will achieve it, so imagining myself where I am happy in what I do and is very satisfied with my life is enough to keep me going.
